# 無尽（むじん）— Overview Presentation
*Rooted in Japan. Built for the world.*

---

## The Problem

**350,000 international students** live and study in Japan. Most are locked out of Japan's financial system — no credit history, no co-signer, no record any bank will recognize.

In 2025, the Business Manager Visa capital requirement jumped from ¥5M to ¥30M. The window to stay and build is closing for students with real potential.

These students are not invisible because they're unworthy. The system was never built for them.

---

## The Opportunity

Japan's Protestant churches are **facing 50% closure by 2030**. International students are arriving at their doors. Tokyo wards are actively seeking community partners under multicultural coexistence frameworks.

These are not separate problems. They are one solution, waiting to be connected.

---

## What Is Mujin?

**A trust-based grant program** that bridges international students from credit-invisible to bank-ready — using Japan's own tradition of mutual aid.

**The name:** 無尽講 (Mujin-ko) — Japan's centuries-old rotating mutual aid circles. Not a foreign import. A revival.

**The model:**
- Donors give to a Religious Corporation (tax-deductible)
- Students receive a ¥500,000 "Venture Scholarship" — a grant, not a loan
- Upon success, students voluntarily return the principal + a covenant gift to fund the next generation
- Capital recycles. The fund grows over time.

**The legal structure:** Religious Corporation (Shukyo Hojin). Non-binding pledge. No debt collection. Structured to stay outside FSA loan classification.

## The Product: Trust Engine

Instead of credit scores, Mujin measures **Social Collateral** — behavior-based signals that are stronger predictors of creditworthiness than financial history.

| Signal | Measure | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| Responsiveness | Bi-weekly mentor check-ins attended | 25% |
| Transparency | Monthly P&L submitted on time | 25% |
| Mutualism | Town Halls attended (peer-reported) | 25% |
| Reflection | Monthly reflection assessed as meaningful | 25% |

**Traffic light output:** Green / Yellow / Red

**Graduation = Bank Introduction.** Four hard gates:
1. Company incorporated + product live
2. 3 months non-negative cash flow
3. Green trust score for 6 consecutive months
4. Exit interview passed

This is how we prove Social Collateral works — and build the case for banks.

## The Physical Layer

Church assets within 15 minutes of major universities become **The Mujin Commons** — a secular co-working space Monday–Friday.

A ¥10,000/month Church Usage Agreement satisfies the Immigration Bureau's **physical office requirement** for the Business Manager Visa.

The church is already the answer. Mujin is the operating model.
